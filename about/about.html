About me
.


Hi my name is Oliver, I'm 18 and from Scotland.
I shoot mostly nature and landscapes, however I am
trying to expand what I shoot with doing more street
photography, portraiture (of my dog) and have recently
tried out some product photography.

Photography is very much a hobby for me that I find an
enjoyable challenge to undertake, I always strive to
capture better and more aesthetically pleasing shots.

I post most of my photos to my Instagram once I have
spent too much time in Lightroom...

Thank you for taking the time to visit my small
showcase of work and I hope you enjoyed!
